An independent record shop in Jakarta, and the platform that runs it.

Black and white, one typeface, and a single mark. Geist sets the words; Geist Mono sets the numbers. Toggle the theme top-right — every value below is live.

01 / COLOUR

Colour

Black and white carry the whole system. There is no brand accent colour — the interactive accent is simply the opposite of the background. A small status set marks stock and order state, and nothing else.

02 / TYPE

Typography

Geist sets every word; Geist Mono sets figures that must align. SemiBold for headings, Medium for emphasis, Regular for body.

03 / BUTTONS

Buttons & chips

One filled button per view points to the main action — white on dark, black on light. Filters and status use pills so they read as a separate family.

04 / CARDS

Cards & signature components

Cards round to 8px and lean on surface contrast plus a hairline, not shadow. The release card and the Now Playing bar carry the brand even without the logo.

05 / FORMS

Form elements

Inputs sit on the raised surface with a 6px radius and an accent focus border. Labels are 12px uppercase mute, above the field.

06 / SPACING

Spacing

Base unit 4px. Card padding 16–20; page gutters 24–32.

07 / RADIUS

Border radius

Mostly soft-square. The pill is reserved for filters and status; cards sit at 8px.

08 / ELEVATION

Elevation

Depth comes from surface steps and hairlines. Shadow appears only on things that float.
