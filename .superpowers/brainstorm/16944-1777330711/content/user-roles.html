User Roles & Permissions

Five roles across two portals — Backoffice (staff) and Storefront (customers/wholesale).

Admin
Full access to everything. Configures integrations, manages staff, and controls all settings.
Backoffice
Store Manager / Purchasing
Full operational access. Manages inventory, POs, orders, and shipping. Cannot change integrations or staff.
Backoffice
Shopkeeper
Processes walk-in POS sales, scans barcodes, views inventory. Cannot edit catalog or access finance.
Backoffice (limited)
Wholesaler
Approved B2B buyer. Sees wholesale catalogue with tiered pricing. Places and tracks their own orders.
Wholesale Portal
Customer
Standard retail buyer. Browses storefront, places orders, tracks deliveries, manages wishlist.
