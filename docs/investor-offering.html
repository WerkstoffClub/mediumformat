Section02 · Scope

What's inside the build. Every module. Every channel.

7 Features · 40+ Deliverables

The build ships as seven features across public storefront, admin back office, in-store POS, marketplace channels, marketing and reporting. Pricing per feature is on page 03; skip-costs are baked into each row.

01 Foundations & Deploy
Required
Framework, database, auth, jobs, design system and deploy pipeline. Nothing else runs without this.
  • Next.js 15 + TypeScript
  • PostgreSQL 16 + Prisma 5
  • Auth.js v5 · 5 roles
  • BullMQ + Redis workers
  • Monochrome v2.0 tokens
  • Docker Compose + nginx + TLS
02 Public Storefront
Website
Common-ground-inspired dark storefront tuned for Jakarta buyers.
  • Homepage & journal
  • Shop / release browser
  • Release detail + tracklist
  • Track previews (Apple / YT / Bandcamp)
  • Cart & guest checkout
  • Xendit payments (QRIS, VA, cards)
  • Biteship shipping quotes
  • Customer accounts & wantlist
03 Admin Back Office
Ops
Single dashboard operating catalog, inventory, orders and staff roles.
  • Dashboard & live KPIs
  • Catalog + Discogs import
  • Inventory & variants
  • Unified order pipeline
  • Customers & wantlist CRM
  • News / journal CMS
  • Unified messages inbox
  • Users, roles, permissions
04 POS & In-Store
Tablet
Same catalog, same stock, same customer records — usable from the counter.
  • Tablet web POS
  • Barcode scanning
  • Label & receipt printing
  • Cash + Xendit terminal
  • Walk-in customer capture
  • Shift & drawer reports
05 Marketplace Sync
3 Channels
One inventory pushed & reconciled across every marketplace.
  • Discogs Marketplace
  • Tokopedia
  • Shopee
  • Webhook order intake
  • Per-channel pricing
  • Sync health monitoring
06 Marketing & Newsletter
AI-assisted
Owned audience. Listmonk-driven newsletter with OpenRouter copy assist.
  • Listmonk newsletter
  • AI copy assist (OpenRouter)
  • Promo & discount codes
  • Segments & sign-up forms
  • Automated new-arrivals digest
  • Wholesale role & pricing
07 Reports & Settings
Insight
Actionable reports and the boring plumbing that makes it all legal.
Sales report Inventory report Channel profitability Tax report (PPN 11%) Store profile Payments & shipping Integrations config Backup & audit log
